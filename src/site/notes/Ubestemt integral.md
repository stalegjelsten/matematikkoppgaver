# Ubestemt integral

Regn ut integralet

$$
\int 4x\sqrt{ x^2+2 }\, dx 
$$

>[!question]- Fasit
>
> $\frac{4}{3}\cdot \sqrt{ x^2+2 } \cdot(x^2+2)+C=\frac{4}{3}(x^2+2)^{\frac{3}{2}}+C$
